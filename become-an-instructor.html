Become a driving instructor

Qualifying in 1998 Nick is experienced in training people to become driving instructors. He has extensive qualifications that include:

  • PTLLS (Preparing to Teach in the Lifelong Learning Sector)
  • CTLLS (Certificate of Teaching in the Lifelong Learning Sector)
  • Assessors award in Training and Development
  • Level 3 NVQ in driving instruction
  • DVSA Approved Driving Instructor
  • ORDIT (Official Register of Driving Instructor Trainers)
  • Fleet Trainer.

He can cater for different learning styles tailoring courses around individual needs using proven techniques in teaching, instructing and coaching. Training is available in the Leeds and York areas with potential to work around other local areas. Nick is patient, professional, flexible with times and days for lessons; and uses a modern tuition vehicle equipped with dual controls.
