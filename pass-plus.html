What is Pass Plus?

Pass Plus is a scheme that was introduced to help new drivers who have recently passed improve their attitudes and skills that are essential for 'safe driving for life'.

The Pass Plus syllabus with checkmirrors

  • Module 1: Introduction (Part 1)
  • Module 1: Town driving (Part 2)
  • Module 2: All-weather driving
  • Module 3: Out of town driving & rural roads
  • Module 4: Night driving
  • Module 5: Dual carriageways
  • Module 6: Motorways

When you have successfully completed the course, you will be issued with a Certificate of Completion.
