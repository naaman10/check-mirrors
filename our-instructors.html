Sarah Hellowell

Sarah is the co founder of checkmirrors.

She is an ADI with qualifications that include The Diamond Special Test, Pass Plus and Cardington Special Test. She has been a fully qualified instructor for over 10 years and takes pride in offering a professional, calm and safe environment that will help you pass your test.

Rob Kedge

Rob has 10 years experience as an ADI. He is a patient and friendly instructor able to adapt to teaching methods to suit each indivdual person. As well as an ADI Rob is a Pass Plus and Fleet trainer.

Nick Lawson

Qualifying in 1998 Nick is experienced in training learners, trainee driving instructors, qualified and experienced drivers and fleet driver training. Past and current qualifications include: PTLLS (Preparing to Teach in the Lifelong Learning Sector), CTLLS (Certificate of Teaching in the Lifelong Learning Sector), Assessors award in Training and Development, Level 3 NVQ in driving instruction, DVSA Approved Driving Instructor, ORDIT (Official Register of Driving Instructor Trainers) and Fleet Trainer. He can cater for different learning styles tailoring courses around individual needs using proven techniques in teaching, instructing and coaching. Training is available in the Leeds and York areas with potential to work around other local areas. Nick is patient, professional, flexible with times and days for lessons; and uses a modern tuition vehicle equipped with dual controls.
