Sarah Hellowell

Sarah is the founder of checkmirrors.

She is an ADI with qualifications that include The Diamond Special Test, Pass Plus and Cardington Special Test. She has been a fully qualified instructor for over 17 years and takes pride in offering a professional, calm and safe environment that will help you pass your test.

Rob Kedge

Rob has 17 years experience as an ADI. He is a patient and friendly instructor able to adapt to teaching methods to suit each indivdual person. As well as an ADI Rob is a Pass Plus and Fleet trainer.

Ryan Fawcett

Ryan has been a qualified driving instructor and has been teaching for over 2 and half years. Ryan is a Business Management graduate who has spent 5 years in sales before deciding to become a qualified driving instructor in York. Ryan enjoys working with all ages and thrives on the challenge and success of helping people pass their tests.
